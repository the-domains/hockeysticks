Holtby shutout lifts Capitals past Flyers in Game 1

WASHINGTON -- Braden Holtby made 19 saves when the Washington Capitals shut out the Philadelphia Flyers 2-0 in Game 1 of the Eastern Conference First Round at Verizon Center on Thursday. Holtby's third career NHL playoff shutout helped the Capitals take a 1-0 lead in the best-of-7 series.
